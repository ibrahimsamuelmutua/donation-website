About Us

UnashamedHeart Foundation Overview

The UnashamedHeart Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to extending a helping hand to those in need within our community. Our mission revolves around fostering positive and lasting change by addressing the various challenges faced by individuals and families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to alleviate the struggles of the less fortunate, providing essential aid and support to meet their basic needs.

Our Vision

The vision of the UnashamedHeart Foundation is to create a society where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and lead a dignified life. We envision a world where the needs of the most vulnerable are met, and where compassion and support are extended to those facing adversity.

Our ambition

The ambition of the UnashamedHeart Foundation is to be a beacon of hope and transformative change in the lives of those experiencing tough economic times. Our Achievements

Visiting Children Homes

So far as the UnshamedHeart Foundation, we have made 150 home visits across Kenya and in each home we've made donations worth Ksh100,000 and above.

Stretching our love to people living with disability

Donated over 520 wheelchairs to the disabled in a span of six months.

Helping Street Children

So far we have managed to help over 500 street children. Some have been taken to rehabilitation centers, others to children homes and others have gone back to school all this because of our foundation.

Door to Door Donation

Conducted door-to-door donation where we have reached out to 1700 elderly families across the country.
